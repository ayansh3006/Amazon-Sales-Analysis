# 📊 Amazon Sales Analysis

An end-to-end **Data Analytics Project** that analyzes Amazon sales data using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**. This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow—from raw data cleaning and exploratory data analysis to SQL-based business analysis and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The objective is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project follows a complete industry-standard analytics workflow:

- Data Cleaning & Inspection
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- SQL Business Analysis
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard
- Business Report with Insights & Recommendations

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across product categories.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate regional sales performance.
- Monitor order fulfillment and delivery status.
- Identify high-performing products and sizes.
- Generate actionable business insights.
- Support strategic business decision-making.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Amazon.in generated the majority of total revenue.
- T-Shirts and Shirts were the highest-performing product categories.
- Medium, Large, and XL sizes contributed the highest sales.
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Telangana generated the highest revenue.
- Amazon Fulfillment processed most customer orders.
- The majority of orders were successfully delivered.
- The business primarily serves B2C customers.
- Monthly sales trends indicate changing customer demand.

---

# 📌 Business Recommendations

- Maintain inventory for high-demand products.
- Increase stock availability for popular sizes.
- Expand marketing in top-performing regions.
- Reduce order cancellations through operational improvements.
- Improve fulfillment efficiency.
- Diversify sales channels.
- Utilize demand forecasting for inventory planning.
